## D. Evidence Gaps (verify or flag before/while drafting)

1. **Lisbon-specific container numbers are thin** — most variety/care targets are extrapolated from citrus-general or container-crop science. Label accordingly in every section.
2. **No 19"×14"-specific trial** — pot-size effects are inferred from depth/AFP and general container studies.
3. **Container-scaled fertilizer rates** — corpus gives field/per-tree N (0.15–0.90 lb N/tree/yr) and EC ceilings (<1.5 dS/m) but no pot-calibrated rate; must be downscaled and stated as derived.
4. **No ready-made fertigation recipe** for citrus in the corpus — Section 10 must adapt IFAS targets + Yara Ca/Mg + chelate-by-pH into a recipe, flagged as constructed.
5. **Local bloom/chill timing** for Lisbon in coastal LA (marine-layer June-gloom) is not measured — calendar is extrapolated from induction physiology.
