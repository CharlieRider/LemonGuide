# 19. Seasonal Irrigation Schedule + Annual Operating Calendar

> **Evidence callout.** This is an **operational assembly** of dated guidance whose evidence
> homes are earlier sections — UC Master Gardener container calendars, UC IPM fertilizing
> timing, CIMIS ET, and the synthesis prevention cycle [D092](#claim-d092), [D016](#claim-d016), [B020](#claim-b020), [B140](#claim-b140). The
> *biological* phenology behind it is §4; irrigation *practice* is §11; nutrition is §10. It is
> **strong on the framework, weak on exact dates** (mild West-LA blurs the calendar — §4).
> **Use it as a default rhythm, overridden by direct observation** (pot weight, pour-through
> test, flush watching).

A container Lisbon is everbearing — **care never fully switches off** (§4) — but inputs still
follow a clear annual arc: **feed and water hard Feb–Oct, ease off in winter, do the heavy
maintenance in late-winter dormancy, and protect against heat in summer and the rare frost in
winter.** The two recurring "do not skip" items are the **per-watering leach** (all year) and
the **2–3-year prevention cycle** (late winter).

## 19.1 Seasonal Irrigation Schedule (by ET, set by observation)

Coastal/West-LA ETo swings **~3–5× across the year** (~5–6 mm/day mid-summer → ~1–2 mm/day
winter) [B020](#claim-b020) — the schedule must track that, not a fixed calendar [B021](#claim-b021), [E025](#claim-e025).

| Season | ETo / demand | Typical frequency (14″ west pot) | Leach every watering | Claims |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Summer (Jun–Sep)** | Peak (~5–6 mm/day) | **~Daily** in heat; more on windy/Santa-Ana days | 15–30% (push ~0.3, hard water) | B020, A103, D097, B069 |
| **Spring / Fall (Mar–May, Oct–Nov)** | Moderate | Every **2–3 days**, weather-driven | 15–30% | B020, A129 |
| **Winter (Dec–Feb)** | Low (~1–2 mm/day) | Every several days–weekly; keep just moist, **never waterlogged** | 15–30% when watering | B020, D091 |

**Always:** water **early morning**, deep to a small drainage surplus, then dry-down; **set
frequency by pot weight / depth check, not the calendar** [B114](#claim-b114), [B112](#claim-b112), [B023](#claim-b023) (§11). Marine-layer
mornings cut demand [B022](#claim-b022). **Monthly deep flush** (2–3× pot volume) year-round [C127](#claim-c127).

## 19.2 Annual Operating Calendar (West-LA / Northern Hemisphere)

| Month(s) | Irrigation | Nutrition | Pruning / structure | Protection | Pest/disease | Claims |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Jan–Feb** (late winter) | Low; just moist | **First feed Jan–Feb before bloom**; start weekly-weakly | **Repot / root-prune + media refresh + deep salt flush (2–3-yr cycle)**; structural prune before flush | Frost watch (mid-20s °F kill) — cover/pre-water/move pot | Scout flush for ACP | D016, D089, D065, B140, B049, E064 |
| **Mar–May** (spring) | Rising to every 2–3 d | Monthly granular **or** weekly-weakly; micros every 4–6 wk; **2nd N at flowering** | Light shaping; pull suckers | Begin heat watch late spring; acclimate any new tree | Post-bloom drop (normal); CLM/ACP on flush; thrips petal-fall→1.5″ | D089, D090, E100, E064, E084 |
| **Jun** (early summer) | ~Daily begins | Continue; **Ca during fruit set (~Apr–Aug)** | — | June drop (normal, don't react); start shade | Spider mites begin (hot) — hose | B032, D090, E082 |
| **Jul–Aug** (summer) | **~Daily**, more in wind | Continue weekly-weakly + micros; **3rd N by mid-June onward** | **No pruning into heat** | **30–50% afternoon shade >95–100°F**; light/double pot; mulch | Spider mites peak; scale/mealybug; orangedog (hand-pick) | A103, E100, B080, E082, E095 |
| **Sep–Oct** (fall) | Tapering to every 2–3 d | **Taper feeding; last feed ~Oct**; no late-season N | Light shaping ok | Ease shade as heat fades | Continue flush scouting | D089, D013, D092 |
| **Nov–Dec** (early winter) | Low; just moist | **Minimal/no feed** | **Prune after winter harvest** (preserves fruit wood) | **Frost watch**; protect hanging fruit (26–28°F) | Reduced pressure | D089, A124, C033 |
| **Year-round** | **Leach 15–30% every watering + monthly deep flush** | — | Remove deadwood/suckers as seen | — | **Scout every flush for ACP; never move the tree; report HLB (CDFA 1-800-491-1899)** | B140, C127, E064, D058 |

## 19.3 The Non-Negotiable Recurring Tasks

| Frequency | Task | Why | Home |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Every watering** | Leach 15–30% (≈daily in summer) | Salt control on hard water | §11, §14 |
| **Weekly (Feb–Oct)** | Weekly-weakly feed + micros | Leaching pot + poor-scavenging dwarf root | §10 |
| **Monthly** | Deep flush (2–3× pot vol); pour-through EC/pH check | Catch salt/pH drift | §14 |
| **Each new flush** | Scout for ACP | HLB is fatal & reportable | §16 |
| **Annually (post-harvest)** | Light canopy prune | Preserve fruit wood, shape | §7 |
| **Every 2–3 yr (late winter)** | Root-prune + media refresh + deep flush | Defeat the 5–7-yr decline cycle | §5, §17 |
| **Once / as needed** | Water-quality test; annual leaf-tissue test | Set leaching + nutrition | §14 |

---

> ### 🪴 Worked example — 19″×14″ west-facing Brentwood / West LA balcony, year in brief
> - **Winter (Dec–Feb):** harvest the main crop; water lightly but **leach every time**; **do the
>   2–3-yr root-prune/refresh/flush in late winter** before the spring flush; **frost-watch** the
>   mid-20s °F nights (cover + pre-water + move pot); first feed Jan–Feb. [§5, §15, §10]
> - **Spring (Mar–May):** ramp watering to every 2–3 days; weekly-weakly feed + micros; light
>   shaping + pull suckers; expect post-bloom drop; scout flush for ACP. [§11, §10, §7, §16]
> - **Summer (Jun–Sep):** **~daily watering by pot weight**, 15–30% leach; **30–50% afternoon
>   shade** above 95–100°F, light/double pot, mulch; **no pruning in heat**; spider-mite watch
>   (hose undersides); June drop is normal. [§11, §15, §16]
> - **Fall (Oct–Nov):** taper water and **stop feeding by ~Oct** (no late-season N); prune after
>   harvest; ease shade. [§11, §10, §7]
> - **All year:** leach every watering, monthly flush + pour-through test, scout every flush,
>   never move the tree. [§14, §16]

## Key takeaways
- **Track ET, set by observation:** ~daily summer water, every several days in winter (3–5×
  swing) — by **pot weight, not calendar** [B020](#claim-b020), [B023](#claim-b023).
- **Feed Feb–Oct weekly-weakly; ease off in winter**; first feed Jan–Feb, no late-season N
  [D089](#claim-d089), [D016](#claim-d016), [D013](#claim-d013).
- **Late winter = heavy maintenance** (root-prune + refresh + flush, structural prune) [B140](#claim-b140), [D065](#claim-d065); **after harvest = light canopy prune** [A124](#claim-a124).
- **Two never-skip recurring tasks:** leach every watering [B140](#claim-b140) and **scout every flush for
  ACP** [E064](#claim-e064).
- **Summer = shade + daily water; winter = frost watch + minimal inputs** [B080](#claim-b080), [D012](#claim-d012).

## Things not to do
- **Don't water or feed on a fixed calendar** — track ET/observation [B021](#claim-b021), [E025](#claim-e025).
- **Don't feed or prune into a heat wave, or feed late-season** — pushes vulnerable growth
  [B080](#claim-b080), [D013](#claim-d013).
- **Don't skip the per-watering leach or the late-winter cycle** — decline follows [B140](#claim-b140), [B139](#claim-b139).
- **Don't stop scouting in winter** — ACP/flush vigilance is year-round [E064](#claim-e064).
- **Don't waterlog in winter** rain/low-demand months [D091](#claim-d091).

## Evidence gaps
- **Exact dates are weak** — the calendar is a default rhythm; mild marine West-LA blurs bloom,
  flush, and harvest timing (§4) [C015](#claim-c015), [C017](#claim-c017), so observation overrides the table.
- **Irrigation frequency is extrapolated** (~daily summer) [A103](#claim-a103), [D097](#claim-d097), not a measured
  container-Lisbon figure; ET swing is regional [B020](#claim-b020), not pot-calibrated.
- **The operating calendar synthesizes container-citrus extension guidance** [D092](#claim-d092), [D089](#claim-d089), not a
  Lisbon-container trial.
